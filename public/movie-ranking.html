The Best Movies by Zub1Wun

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Bladerunner

I have been watching this again and again since it came out. I never seem to get bored of watching it again. It's just so beautifully shot, every scene and dialogue is perfected. The perfect sequel to the original.

My Neighbour Tortoro

I've watched this countless times with my children, again I never seem to get bored of this. The innocence, the imagination and artistry in this is soo ahead of its time.

Avengers: Infinity Wars

Perhaps the best culmination of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. After this and it's sequal Endgame, it seems to have lost it's magic.

Some honourable mentions

Miasto 44

A Polish historical depicting the Polish resistances final and longest fight against the Nazi Occupation.

La Haine

A French movie about three young friends living in the Ghettoes around Paris.

Malcolm X

An autobiographical film starring Denzel Washington as Malcolm X.

JFK

One of the Oliver Stone's greatest works.
